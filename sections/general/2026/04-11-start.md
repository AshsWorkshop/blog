Hello, hello! And welcome to the first blog post of Ash's Workshop: a place where I just talk about things that interest me.

<!-- truncate -->

I've been wanting to do this for a while now, but I have been putting it off since I never have the time from all my other work. Last time I calculated, I write around 2 million words a year across it all. Because of that, I often forget or cite things from an outdated or too newly dated reference.

So, I'm using this blog as a place to keep track of my ideas and explain my reasoning for why I did things. This can be anything from the concepts I program to the books I write to the music I create. It's all a first draft, meaning grammar and diction errors abound, but it will take the least time.

And hey, if you're not me (which I imagine you aren't), maybe you will be able to gain some insight from what I have to say. Or maybe you're just here to be confused with me. Completely reasonable.

Well, whatever it may be, welcome to my workshop. Feel free to pop in whenever.
